Inbound Order Status Call – Workflow
Verify caller identity — Confirm contact name and account number or company name
Pull up customer record — Search by name, company, or account number in the lookup above
Identify the PO or order in question — Customer should provide their PO number; confirm order status
Review shipment status — Check carrier, tracking number, and last event on the Shipments tab
Check for exceptions or holds — Look for delivery exceptions, on-hold reasons, or delay flags
Communicate status to customer — Provide ETA, tracking link if available, and next steps
Document the interaction — Log notes in Genesys Cloud; note any escalations or commitments made
Offer resolution or escalation — If issue cannot be resolved, escalate to sales rep or logistics team
Select Escalation Reason:
🚨 Order on hold – credit or pricing issue
⏳ Significant delivery delay – customer impacted
📦 Damaged or missing shipment claim
😠 Customer escalation – dissatisfied caller
🔁 Request live supervisor transfer

Opening
Answer
"Thank you for calling Composites One Customer Service, this is [Your Name]. How can I help you today?"
Verify Identity
"May I have your name and your company name or account number so I can pull up your record?"
Order Status Inquiry
Request PO
"Do you have your PO number handy, or do you know the approximate order date? That'll help me locate it quickly."
Confirm Status
"I'm showing your order [PO#] is currently [status]. It was shipped via [carrier] on [ship date], with an estimated delivery of [date]."
Provide Tracking
"Your tracking number with [carrier] is [tracking#]. You can track it directly at [tracking URL], and the last update we have is: [last tracking event]."
Exception / Delay Handling
Delay
"I can see there's been a delay on this shipment — the reason noted is [delay reason]. The revised estimated delivery is [date]. I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience."
On Hold
"I'm showing this order is currently on hold due to [on hold reason]. I'll need to loop in our [accounts receivable / sales] team to get this resolved. Can I arrange a callback, or would you like to hold while I transfer you?"
Partial Shipment
"Your order has been partially fulfilled — [X] of [Y] items have shipped. The remaining [Z] items have an estimated ship date of [date]. I'll make sure you receive tracking when they go out."
Closing
Wrap-Up
"Is there anything else I can help you with today regarding your order or account?"
Close
"Thank you for calling Composites One. We appreciate your business, and please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything further. Have a great day!"
